Pizza Recipe

Description

This set of foods when combined together will produce a really tasty cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Bat and Mix The Dough Into Pizza Shape
  2. Spread The Tomato Sauce Evenly On The Dough
  3. Add the Cheese On Top Of Tomato Sauce
  4. Add The Garlic and Pepper Over The Cheese
  5. Bake In Oven Until Ready
