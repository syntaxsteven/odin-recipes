Tacos Recipe

Description

This is a classic mexican dish which is my favorite kind of taco.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Chicken
  • Tomato
  • Orion
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Wash and clean the chicken
  2. Season the chicken up
  3. Begin to cook the chicken however you would like
  4. Warm up tortillas
  5. Place chicken inside of tortillas once ready
  6. Finish preparing the chicken tacos by adding things should as tomato, orion, and cheese
  7. Dish is ready to be served
